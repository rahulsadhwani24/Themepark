    GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS:

    1. Park your car / bike / bus in designated parking area.
    2. Follow the rules for the usage of the rides. Not following rules can lead to accident / injury.
    3. No outside food / beverages are allowed inside the park premises.
    4. Entry inside the park premises under the influence of alcohol or toxicant is not allowed. If found the guest shall not be allowed or asked to leave the park premises, without any refund / reimbursement.
    5. Alcoholic drinks / drugs strictly prohibited to be carried on person inside the premises. It is an offense.
    6. Please do not leave valuables unattended. The Park Management is not liable for the same at any given point of time.
    7. Amusement rides are body active participatory activities. Such activities present a risk of injury.
    8. Guests with any medical or health restrictions on their physical activity should not use Amusement Rides /Attractions.
    9. Look at each ride and see if you can safely participate - you are the best Judge of your limitations.
    10. Please supervise your children and arrange a meeting place in case you should get separated.
    11. Children under 12 years old must be accompanied by an adult.
    12. We reserve the right to stop you from taking pictures or recording videos.
    13. Smoking is not allowed inside the Park.
    14. Do not spit.
    15. Do not pluck flowers.
    16. Do not throw food / paper / plastic. Use garbage bins provided in the premises.
    17. Do not wash clothes in toilets.
    18. The area may be wet and slippery. Running around shall be risky.
    19. Do not splash water on unknown persons.
    20. Do not indulge in eve teasing / passing lewd remarks in front or to ladies and in presence of families. Security has rights to remove you from the premises, without giving you any reason whatsoever.
    21. Do not indulge in boisterous behaviour that will disturb other guests. Enjoy & let others enjoy.
